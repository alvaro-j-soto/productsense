Suggested Customer-Facing Language
Instead of: “Our TEDS data layer ensures canonical employee identity”
Say: “TeamSense keeps one clean record for every employee across all your systems”
Instead of: “Our extensible data model supports bi-directional HRIS sync”
Say: “TeamSense stays in sync with Workday, UKG, and ADP automatically”
Instead of: “Our AI agentic platform orchestrates actions”
Say: “When someone calls out, TeamSense can suggest the best replacement and handle the outreach”
Instead of: “We have a platform with shared services”
Say: “When you add Communicate, it works instantly — same employees, same permissions, no setup”
